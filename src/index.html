Our T&N Mattress cover is made with a blend of Polyester and Tactel (nylon). It has a soft feel which is very pleasant to the touch. Tactel is commonly used in clothing for men, women and children as an alternative to cotton. It is also known to dry 6-8 times faster than cotton, which helps you sleep cooler, longer. With durability in mind, we've designed a fabric that will last. Our Mattress cover fabric is pilling and tearing resistant. Our T&N Mattress cover has been tested and certified by OEKO-TEX®. OEKO-TEX® tests for harmful substances in textiles and materials. The more intensive the skin contact of a product and the more sensitive the skin, the stricter the human-ecological requirements that need to be complied with.
We are proud to hold the STANDARD 100 by OEKO-TEX®: Class I certification on the T&N Mattress cover. While our mattress was not designed for babies and toddlers, the STANDARD 100 by OEKO-TEX®: Class I certification signifies that all parts of an article meet the OEKO-TEX® requirements for a product used by babies and toddlers up to 3 years of age. U.S. law requires that every mattress sold needs to pass two very specific burn tests: an "open flame" test and a "cigarette" test.

In order to pass these two tests, a mattress typically needs to have a component known as a fire barrier that self extinguishes the mattress when on fire.
